About Us:

Dignity Unite Screening is a trusted provider of compassionate, professional, and reliable mobile phlebotomy and health screening services. With a focus on patient-centered care, the organization is committed to making healthcare more accessible, dignified, and efficient for individuals and communities alike.

By combining innovation, professionalism, and unwavering dedication, Dignity Unite Screening redefines convenience and excellence in diagnostic services—empowering clients with timely, accurate, and respectful care delivered right to their doorstep.

Our Mission:

Dignity Unite Screening is dedicated to enhancing lives through compassionate and high-quality mobile phlebotomy and screening services. Our mission is to deliver accessible healthcare solutions that prioritize dignity, efficiency, and accuracy—ensuring every client feels valued, respected, and cared for.

Meet Our Founder

Janelle Henry

Janelle is the founder of Dignity Unite Screening and brings over 17 years of experience in the medical field. Her professional background includes work across a wide range of healthcare settings, including physicians’ offices, nursing homes, paramedical insurance examinations, and specialty laboratory collections. She also served as a regional phlebotomy trainer and educator for four years, where she played a key role in developing talent and promoting best practices in patient care.

Her extensive experience has shaped a strong commitment to accessible, compassionate, and patient-centered service. Janelle established Dignity Unite Screening to offer a respectful and welcoming environment where individuals of all backgrounds receive high-quality care with professionalism and empathy.

Holding a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology, Janelle integrates a holistic understanding of physical and emotional well-being into every aspect of the organization’s operations. Her leadership is driven by a belief that healthcare should be not only efficient but also human-centered—ensuring that every client feels valued, heard, and supported throughout their experience.
